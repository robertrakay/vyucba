## üß≠ Course Overview
Brief description of Electrotechnics and Electrical Engineering.  
The course provides fundamental knowledge of electrical quantities, circuits, and measurement techniques essential for automation and control engineering.

**Learning Outcomes:**
- Understand electrical laws and relationships  
- Analyze DC and AC circuits  
- Perform laboratory measurements using standard instruments  
- Apply theoretical knowledge in practical scenarios
